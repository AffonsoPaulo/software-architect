# Business Analysis Checklist

- [ ] `docs/02-business-analysis/business-analysis.md` exists
- [ ] Every `BR-XXX` in the front-matter has a matching entry in the "Business rules" section
- [ ] Every `BR-XXX` is unique and correctly formatted
- [ ] Every business process has at least one identified actor
- [ ] Every business rule is a genuine policy/constraint, not a restated process step
- [ ] The justification reflects the user's own stated reasoning
- [ ] Actors are business roles, not UX personas
- [ ] Every process and rule was explicitly confirmed by the user
