# Discovery Checklist

- [ ] `docs/01-discovery/vision.md` exists with every required section present
- [ ] The problem is stated without ambiguity and is a genuine problem, not a disguised solution
- [ ] Target audience/stakeholders are identified specifically
- [ ] At least one success criterion is genuinely measurable
- [ ] "Out of scope" was explicitly asked about, not left empty by omission
- [ ] No functional-requirement-level content leaked into the Vision document, including inside "User journey" (Fully Dressed)
- [ ] "Guiding principles" (Fully Dressed), if present, are concrete, not generic platitudes
- [ ] Every section was explicitly confirmed by the user
